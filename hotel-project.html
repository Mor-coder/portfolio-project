Do you remember the last time you booked a holiday? Dit it all go as smooth as silk or were there any hiccups?

Maybe a little miscommunication with the hotel, or a discrepancy between the room shown online and the reality of it, or the different perception of "short distance" to the local amnesties - which ends up in you purchasing an unexpected new pair of shoes because the sole of yours is gone...

The point is that, sometimes, there can be unnecessary hiccups during the booking process that turn the dream of a holiday into a frustrating experience. Other times there isn't much anyone can do about the hiccup so you zen out and carry on.

With this in mind, if I were to build a hotel tomorrow, how would I design the booking process to provide a kick-ass experience?


When you don't know, research - When you do know, research anyway for an extra boost of empathy and inspiration.

Objective

Mor.e Contemporary was born from the desire to show the projects and collaborations I have worked on together with a varied group of artists. As the identity of Mor.e Contemporary changed over time, one of the main difficulties I encountered was having an honest and unfiltered conversation with myself. In other words, to truly understand what I wanted to gain out of it, what I wanted to show, and how I intended to do it.

Who's my target audience?

Having worked in the art industry for some times, I knew that my target audience could have been quite variegated. Although it is a bit of a stereotype, it is true that "art people" do not tend to be the most tech-friendly ones and so a clear, straight forward, and minimal design was necessary to make their interaction with the website nice and friendly.

Obviously there are exceptions like art people working with digital media whose knowledge and ability to navigate any digital platform goes above and beyond the imaginary.

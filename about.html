Who's that girl?

SPOILER ALERT: This story contains very human and very intense experiences. Or at least it felt like it at the time, then I grew older and understood that it is all quite common and natural. It doesn't have an end (yet) but you can rest assured it is full of trials and errors, certainties and doubts, ups and downs, and so on and so forth...

Some people seem to know exactly what they want to do in life. Well, that person wasn't me. I wanted to do so many things I didn't even know where to start but, above all, I wanted to do something that would make me happy and satisfied.

My academic adventure kicked off with a BA in interactive communication at the Interdisciplinary Center Herlziya (ISR), where the door to the fascinating world of human-machine interaction has been opened in front of me.

That was mindblowing and yet, I still felt like I was missing something so I move to the smoky London looking for furtune. There I approached the creative, yet structured, world of graphic design which led me to what it would be a full immersion into the art galleries circuit.

Since then it has been a roller coster of experiences covering varied roles within the art world. Despite the wonderful experience, I was missing the challenge of trying to solve human problems with the help of technologies, started in my college years.

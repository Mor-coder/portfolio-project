My story

SPOILER ALERT: this is not a traditional success story, on the contrary, is the story of someone who, through trial and error, found her way.

Some people seem to know exactly what they want to do in life, at least career wise. Well, that person wasn't me. I wanted to do everything but most of all, I wanted to do something that would make me feel satisfied with myself.

After a BA in interactive communication at the Interdisciplinary Center Herlziya, where I gained great knowledge on the interaction between people and technologies (including media), I still felt like I was missing something. Hence the move to the smoky London.
